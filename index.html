Summary: Latitude vs X

This page and the analysies displayed on it are meant to help visualize how weather changes at various distances from the equator. To that end, the weather data for various randomly selected cities was recorded and plotted against their latitudes.There are plots for max temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed along with a short explanation of each. Additionally, there is a page comparing all four plots and another displaying the full table of data gathered for use in these plots. Navigate to whichever page you want using the links above.
