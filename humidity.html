Humidity

The plot on this page displays the correlation of a city's latitude to the humidity it experienced on the particular date the data was gathered. Humidity and latitudinal location do not appear strongly correlated at all. Citites any distance from the equater are just as able to have high or low humidity as any other distance. Of the two extremes to humidity, a city is much more likely to be uncomfortably humid (60-100%) than uncomfortably dry (0-20%)
