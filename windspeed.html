Wind Speed

The plot on this page displays the correlation of a city's latitude to the windspeed on the particular date the data was gathered. Wind speed does not seem to be strongly correlated with latitude, as locations experiencing very low and higher windspeeds can be found at any location. Furthermore, the vast majority of locations experience wind speeds of less than 15mph on any given day, and those that break this barrier are still spread all over every latitude, though noticeably wind speeds of over 25mph only occurred in this dataset in cities farther into the northern hemisphere.
